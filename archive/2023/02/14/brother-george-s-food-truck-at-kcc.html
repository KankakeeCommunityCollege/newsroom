Brother George's BBQ will be bringing their food truck to KCC on Friday, Feb. 17 from noon - 2 p.m. They will be parked in south lot near the wind tower. Food will be available for purchase.

Anyone from the community are welcome. The food truck visit is being held during Black History Month as a way to supporting a Black owned and operated business!

The event was planned by Catholic Charities, which has it's Kankakee area offices on KCC's West Campus.

Beginning April 24, the KCC cafeteria hours will be as follows:

Monday - Thursday: 8 a.m. - 3 p.m.
Fridays: 9 a.m. - 1 p.m. (when the college is open)

The cafeteria website has additional information about dining choices, services and mobile ordering.

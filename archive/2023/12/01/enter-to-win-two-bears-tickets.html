The KCC Veterans Association is raffling off two tickets to the Chicago Bears vs. Arizona Cardinals game on Christmas Eve! 

Raffle tickets are $4 each, or 3 for $10. All proceeds will help send club members to the Student Veteran Association National Conference in January 2024.

Tickets will be sold in the KCC College Center on Monday, Dec. 4 from 11 a.m. – 1 p.m., or will be available for purchase from Megan Ponton in Room L205. She can be reached at mponton@kcc.edu or 815-802-8553 for any questions.

The winner will be drawn on Thursday, Dec. 14!

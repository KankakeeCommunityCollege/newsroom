​A run/walk organized in memory of Bradley Police Department’s Sgt. Marlene Rittmanic and in honor of Officer Tyler Bailey will be May 22.

It's the "I Got Your Six 5K Run / 2-Mile Walk."

All proceeds from this event will be used to fund two KCC Law Enforcement Scholarships in each officer’s name. 

  • Date and time: Sunday, May 22, 8 a.m.
  • Location: Perry Farm Park, 459 N Kennedy Drive, Bourbonnais. The event will be on a smooth paved path that winds through natural prairie, woods, and riverfront.  
  • Participation options: 5K, 2 mile walk, 5K (virtual), 2 mile walk (virtual).

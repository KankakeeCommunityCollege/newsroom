Single moms are invited to sign up for a free car wash and oil change for “Single Mom Saturday.”

To participate:  sign up on the WBGL website during August. The station will contact you back for a scheduled time for your car on the morning of Saturday, Sept. 22 from 8 a.m. - noon at Court Street Ford, 558 William Latham Sr. Drive, Bourbonnais.

This event is not sponsored by KCC. It is being posted as a service to the community.

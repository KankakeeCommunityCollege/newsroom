All members of the community are asked to go online to 2020Census.gov and complete the U.S. census questionnaire.

Those who need assistance can call 1-844-330-2020.

The census also has a Help/Frequently Asked Questions page to help people who have questions as well as understand why your answers matter.

This message is being posted as a public service.

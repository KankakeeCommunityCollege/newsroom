The Continuing Education Department at Kankakee Community College is offering a series of courses called the Leadership & Management Institute.

The series has one class per month, and each class meets from 9 a.m. - 1 p.m. at KCC in Room D124. All sessions are taught by Dr. Kris Condon.

The courses are “Effective Time Management,” Wednesday, Jan 15; “Communicating with Impact” Wednesday, Feb. 19; “Working as a High-Performing Team,” Wednesday, March 18; “Addressing Poor Performance,” Wednesday, April 15; and “Valuing Differences, ” Wednesday, May 20.

Course descriptions are online at www.kcc.edu/comejoinus. Cost for each class is $150. The cost for all five courses for those who register before Jan. 15 is $700, a savings of $50.

The courses do not award college credit. Online registration is available. At kcc.edu/comejoinus, sign up or login, then select the course, click on the date and add it to your cart. Within the cart, checkout. To register by phone, or for more information, phone 815-802-8207.

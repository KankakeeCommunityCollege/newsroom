What is PTSD?
A condition that can occur after a person has experienced a traumatic event involving intense fear and threat of bodily injury or death. Examples include military combat, sexual assault, or natural disasters.
Source: Very Well Mind

Treatment options

Trauma-focused psychotherapies are the most highly recommended type of treatment for PTSD. These therapies are:

  • Prolonged exposure
  • Cognitive processing therapy
  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing

Source: National Center for PTSD

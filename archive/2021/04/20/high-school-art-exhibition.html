The 32nd annual KCC High School Art Exhibition is now online for viewing.

“Although the show is online, it is still a beautiful representation of our local talent, and it is exciting to see it all in one place,” said Chelise Slowik, KCC art professor.

Over 100 students from 11 different high schools are participating! 

The schools represented are: Pontiac High School, Herscher High School, Bishop McNamara Catholic High School, Bradley-Bourbonnais Community High School, Grant Park High School, Cissna Park High School, Watseka High School, Manteno High School, Manteno High School, Kankakee High School and Momence High School.

Send us your photos participating in each theme day at nschunke@kcc.edu by 3 p.m. for chances to WIN a $50 Amazon® Gift Card at the end of each day.

Monday, May 10 – Spirit Day! Wear your Cavaliers Spirit wear, or something that is scarlet or navy.

Tuesday, May 11 – Celebrate your program! Represent your program or where you plan to transfer.

Wednesday, May 12 – Give a shout-out! Show off a sign or message to family friends, faculty and staff who made a difference during your college journey.

Thursday, May 13 – Alumni Day! Proud alums: Send us your photos of you on the job.

Friday, May 14 – Grad Cap Swag! Share a photo of your decorated cap for the big day!

Saturday, May 15 – Commencement! Watch on: commencement.kcc.edu

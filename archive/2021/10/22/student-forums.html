KCC students are encouraged to attend forums to meet candidates for vice president for student affairs. Each candidate will share his/her bio, what attracted him/her to this position at KCC, and will answer questions.

Each forum is from 1:30-2 p.m. The dates and locations are:

  • Tuesday, Oct. 26 – Iroquois Room, Room D152

  • Wednesday, Oct. 27 – Workforce Development Center Conference Hall, Room D140

  • Wednesday, Nov. 3 – Iroquois Room, Room D152

Student input will be important as we choose someone for this student-centered position.

Refreshments will be offered!

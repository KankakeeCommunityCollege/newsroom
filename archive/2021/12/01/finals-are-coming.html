KCC's Student Affairs Department offers numerous resources and services to get through the semester. They include study tips and tools for stress/anxiety management. Choose from any of the following links to find resources appropriate for your needs.

Relieving stress / managing test anxiety

  • What test anxiety is and how to reduce it
  • Create a weekly planner to schedule studying and space study time into chunks. This site also includes links to a word doc and PDF of a schedule to utilize.
  • Relaxation strategies
  • Video featuring effective stress relievers (the video is embedded on the page)

Study tips to conquer finals

  1. Create your own study guide
  2. Ask questions
  3. Attend review sessions
  4. Know your final exam schedule and start ahead schedule
  5. Teach back the material to peers
  6. Revolve your focus, switching between subjects is a helpful way to learn and prevent burnout on one topic
  7. Utilize platforms like YouTube for visual/auditory learners

Area kids are invited to Juneteenth Story Time at 5:30 p.m. on Thursday June 17, 2021,  at the Kankakee Public Library, 2nd Floor.

The FREE event is for kids ages 3-12 to come and listen to a story about the Juneteenth holiday and have fun making a festive craft to take home.

The Kankakee Public Library is at 201 E. Merchant St., Kankakee.

To watch the story time event live online using Zoom: go to bit.ly/Kankakee-Juneteenth.

To RSVP for the in-person event, or for additional information, contact Whitney Mittons, wmittons@kcc.edu. Mittons is coordinator of equity, diversity and inclusion at Kankakee Community College. 

The event is sponsored by the Center for Equity, Diversity and Inclusion at KCC and the Kankakee Public Library.

The Center for Equity, Diversity and Inclusion at KCC also has addition information about Juneteenth on its website.

The Kankakee Community College Alumni Association will host a Beer Tasting Event at Steam Hollow Brewery in Manteno on Thursday, Oct. 10.

KCC alumni, friends and family of alumni are invited. The event, from 5:30-7:30 p.m. on Oct. 10 will include a flight of four beers to sample, a commemorative beer stein, plus other fun. Crawlers will be available for purchase. Steam Hollow Brewing is at 450 S. Spruce St. Unit C in Manteno.

The cost is $25 per person, and tickets are available only to those age 21 or older. Registration is on the KCC Foundation Giving page. All proceeds benefit KCC Alumni Association Scholarships. 

For more information about the event, contact Jennifer Zimmerman at jzimmerman@kcc.edu or phone 815-802-8251.

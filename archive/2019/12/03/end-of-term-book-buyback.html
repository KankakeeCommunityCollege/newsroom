The KCC Hammes Bookstore will hold book buyback during final exam week, Dec. 9-16, 2019 in the Iroquois Room, Room D152.

Hours are Monday through Thursday, 8:30 a.m. - 6 p.m.; and Friday 8:30 a.m. - 1 p.m. Hours on Dec. 16 will be 8:30 a.m. - 3 p.m.

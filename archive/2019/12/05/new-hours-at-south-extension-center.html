KCC’s Miner South Extension Center, 1488 E. Walnut St. in Watseka, has adjusted its hours for December and early January.

From Dec. 5 to Jan. 10, the center will be open Monday – Thursday, 8:30 a.m. - 7 p.m. There will be extended registration hours from 10 a.m. - 2 p.m. on Saturday, Jan. 11. 

Regular hours will resume on Monday, Jan. 13.

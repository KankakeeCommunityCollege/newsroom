The 2020-21 KCC Foundation Scholarship Application is open!

Current, incoming and prospective students should visit foundation.kcc.edu/scholarships to apply. The application is used for more than 200 available scholarships.  The deadline to apply is March 1, 2020.  

Scholarship recipients will be contacted in May using the email address provided on the application.

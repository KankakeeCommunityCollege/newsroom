The Office of Financial Aid will be hosting "FREE Food & FAFSA" workshops the week of September 16-20. Stop by to learn more about resources to pay for college! Our staff will be available to help students with applications. All students and parents are welcome. Each day will feature FREE food from local businesses!

Below is the schedule of workshops:

* Monday, Sept. 16 from 11:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. - Mancino's Monday
* Tuesday, Sept. 17 from 11:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. - Taco Tuesday with Dos Amigos
* Wednesday, Sept. 18 from 11:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. - Great Wall Wednesday
* Thursday, Sept. 19 from 11:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. - Thirsty Thursday with Essential Smoothies & Jimmy Johns
* Friday, Sept. 20 from 11:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m. - TGIFridays!

For more information, visit the [Financial Aid](https://www.kcc.edu/tuition-and-aid/financial-aid/ "KCC Financial Aid page"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"} page on the KCC website.

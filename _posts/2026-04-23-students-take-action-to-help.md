More than 15 KCC students and staff participated in the 2026 United Way Day of Action for Earth Day.

The group took part in cleanup and beautification at the Pregnancy Resource Center in Kankakee.

The students represented TRIO Student Support Services; the Center for Equity, Diversity & Inclusion; and KCC Kankakee Community College Student Life.

KCC's [Agriculture & Horticulture Program](https://catalog.kcc.edu/degrees?search=&amp;program=1862&amp;type=All "Agriculture &amp; Horticulture Program"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"} is the Innovator in Agriculture for the 2026 Daily Journal Progress Awards.<br /><br />KCC provides hands-on learning, internships, industry connections, and community engagement so students can prepare for careers in agriculture and horticulture. <br /><br />Class topics include [greenhouse operations](https://catalog.kcc.edu/agri-hort/hort-1173 "greenhouse operations"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}; aquaponics; [cultivating plants](https://catalog.kcc.edu/agri-hort/hort-1023 "cultivating plants"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}, [fruits and vegetables](https://catalog.kcc.edu/agri-hort/hort-1323 "fruits and vegetables"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}; [soil science](https://catalog.kcc.edu/agri-hort/agrc-1624 "soil science"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}; [landscape design](https://catalog.kcc.edu/agri-hort/hort-1154 "landscape design"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}; [animal science](https://catalog.kcc.edu/agri-hort/agrc-1704 "animal science"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}; [pest management](https://catalog.kcc.edu/agri-hort/aght-1013 "pest management"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}; and [farmsteading](https://catalog.kcc.edu/agri-hort/aght-1103 "farmsteading"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.<br /><br />The Progress Awards are sponsored by the Kankakee County Chamber of Commerce and The Daily Journal.

To learn more about the program, contact Katelynn Ohrt, program director, at kohrt@kcc.edu or 815-802-8859.

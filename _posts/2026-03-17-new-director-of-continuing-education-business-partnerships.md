Cesar Avalos is the new director of Continuing Education and Business Partnerships at Kankakee Community College.

![Cesar Avalos, director of Continuing Education and Business Partnerships at Kankakee Community College](/uploads/2023/cesaravalos-450x380.jpg "Cesar Avalos, director of Continuing Education and Business Partnerships at Kankakee Community College"){: width="450" height="380"}
{: .center-this-text}

In this role, Avalos will oversee KCC’s customized training and continuing education area, which serve business, industry, public service and community members. He will also work directly with local employers to build partnerships which strengthen the skills of both current and potential employees.

Avalos has a bachelor’s degree in human resource management from American Military University System. He is a veteran of the U.S. Army, with 20 years of experience in in strategic planning, talent acquisition, team leadership, workforce development, career counseling, and community engagement. At KCC, Avalos was previously the assistant director of work-based learning and coordinator of career services.

Each year, about 1,500 people complete non-credit workforce training and professional development at KCC. While these seminars and workshops do not offer college credit, many—including those in health care, social services, and education—qualify for continuing education or professional development units. Other programs lead to industry-recognized credentials, such as CPR Healthcare Provider and Food Safety Manager Certification.

More information about KCC’s Continuing Education and Business Partnerships options are at [www.kcc.edu/continuinged](http://www.kcc.edu/continuinged).

Avalos can be reached at [cavalos@kcc.edu](mailto:cavalos@kcc.edu) or 815-802-8204.

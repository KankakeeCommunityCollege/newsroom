**2026–27 Monetary Award Program (MAP).**

&nbsp;

Due to funding limitations, **the Illinois Student Assistance Commission (ISAC) will suspend MAP award announcements for applications received after April 23, 2026**. Students who submit a FAFSA or Alternative Application after this date will be placed on a waiting list for MAP funding. If additional funds become available, awards will be offered in the order applications were received.

&nbsp;

Students are strongly encouraged to complete a [FAFSA](http://www.fafsa.gov/) or [Alternative Application](https://www.isac.org/alternative-application) as soon as possible. While MAP funding may be limited, eligible students may still qualify for federal Pell Grants and other state, institutional, or scholarship opportunities.

&nbsp;

KCC’s Financial Aid Office is available to assist with completing the FAFSA or Alternative Application between the hours of 8am-5pm Monday-Friday and is located on the 2nd floor in Student Affairs.

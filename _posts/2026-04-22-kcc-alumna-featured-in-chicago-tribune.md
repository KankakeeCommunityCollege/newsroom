**Alumna Featured in** ***Chicago Tribune***

Former KCC student Skylar Stevenson’s artwork has been highlighted in a *Chicago Tribune* article previewing an upcoming art festival.

Stevenson was selected for the Emerging Artists Pavilion at the Elmhurst Art Museum’s annual [*Art in Wilder Park*](https://elmhurstartmuseum.org/events/art%20in%20wilder%20park%202026 "Art in Wilder Park 2026"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}, taking place May 2 and 3. She is one of five artists chosen for this opportunity to exhibit and sell their work.

Fans are invited to wear scarlet and navy for “Pack the Place” basketball games on Thursday, Dec. 4 at Kankakee Community College.

The Lady Cavaliers play Illinois Central College at 5:30 p.m., and the men play Daley College at 7:30 p.m. Both games will be in the George H. Ryan Gymnasium at KCC. Admission is free and there will also be FREE refreshments while supplies last.

For questions or more information about the Pack the Place events, contact Maurice Sullivan at [msullivan@kcc.edu](mailto:msullivan@kcc.edu). More information on Cavaliers athletics is at [www.kcc.edu/athletics](http://www.kcc.edu/athletics).

KCC is at 100 College Drive in Kankakee. Directions are on [KCC’s website](http://www.kcc.edu/about/visitor-information).

After a shipping delay, the new dates and times for cap and gown pickup are:

* **Monday-Thursday, May 11-14**
* **9 a.m.-5 p.m.**
* KCC Room L200, Student Affairs near the Financial Aid counter

We apologize for any inconvenience.

Any graduate with questions is welcome to contact Student Affairs at [815-802-8509](tel:+18158028509) or email [commencement@kcc.edu](mailto:commencement@kcc.edu).

## Make it look great

As always, we offer this advice/options to have your gown looking its best for commencement:

* Place it on a hanger to allow the folds to fall out, preferably in a high-humidity area.
  * If it is still wrinkled, iron your gown. Turn it inside out and press with a warm—not hot—iron.
* Steaming is also an option.

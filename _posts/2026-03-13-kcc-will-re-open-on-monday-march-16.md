All Kankakee Community College facilities will reopen and resume normal operations on Monday, March 16, following the recent tornado and severe storms. Classes will resume as scheduled, and all offices will be open.

We appreciate your patience and understanding as we continue addressing some temporary repairs. While permanent repairs are underway, there is a possibility that minor issues, such as leaks, may occur.

The college is grateful for the support shown by our community during this time and looks forward to welcoming everyone back.

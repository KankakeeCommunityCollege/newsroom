KCC's Hammes Bookstore will be closed for Inventory from Wednesday, June 24 to Wednesday, July 1.

Students should note that June 15 is the final day to make purchases using financial aid.

Although the college online bookstore at [books.kcc.edu](){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"} will be accessible, no orders will be sent between June 24 and July.

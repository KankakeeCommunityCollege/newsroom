**Survey link:** [**https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/KankakeeWorkforceServices**](https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/KankakeeWorkforceServices%20 "Kankakee Workforce Services survey"){: target="_blank" rel="noopener"}
